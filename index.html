Know the Difference

Psychiatrist vs Psychologist

Help people understand which professional they need

Psychiatrist

  • ‚úì Medical doctor (MBBS, MD Psychiatry)
  • ‚úì Can prescribe medications
  • ‚úì Treats medical conditions
  • ‚úì Best for: Clinical depression, bipolar, ADHD, OCD, severe anxiety

Psychologist

  • ‚úì Mental health professional (MA/MSc)
  • ‚úì Provides talk therapy
  • ‚úì Focuses on coping strategies
  • ‚úì Best for: Stress, relationships, life transitions, mild anxiety

Many people benefit from both. At Rocket Health, psychiatrists and psychologists work together.
